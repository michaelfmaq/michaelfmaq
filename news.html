Penguin has released the much-anticipated book, What I Did Not Learn in B-School, written by the Founder and CEO of MAQ Software, Rajeev Agarwal. The book bridges the gap between the shortage of qualified managers and the employability of recent MBA graduates.

Every year, top performers join management ranks in their companies. As they assume their new roles, managers often receive inadequate training on adopting the right mindsets and behaviors to succeed.
