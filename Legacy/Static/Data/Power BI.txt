    <p class="DataSubContent">Microsoft Power BI and Amazon QuickSight are rapidly disrupting the visual insights landscape.</p>
	<p class="DataSubContent">Microsoft Power BI enables you to find and visualize data, share discoveries, and collaborate in intuitive new ways. Power BI transforms your company's data into rich visuals for you to collect and organize so you can focus on what matters to you.</p>
