Recognitions

Eight Time Honoree, Inc. 5000, Fastest Growing Companies in America

MAQ Software is honored to be listed on the prestigious Inc. 5000 list for the 8th time, a rare achievement. Since 2007, Inc. magazine has recognized 20,263 unique companies on its Inc. 5000 list based on the sales growth of the companies. Less than 2% of Inc. 5000 companies demonstrate sustained growth by appearing 8 or more times.

For more than 30 years, Inc.’s list has served as evidence of the significant accomplishments of enterprises such as Intuit, Zappos, Under Armor, Microsoft, Jamba Juice, Timberland, Visa, Clif Bar, Patagonia, and Oracle.

ISO 27001:2013 – Information Security

MAQ Software was certified ISO 27001:2013 by British Standards Institution.

ISO 27001 standards, the only auditable international standard, provides an Information Security Management Systems (ISMS) model for adequate and proportionate security controls to protect information assets.

Microsoft Gold Partner

Microsoft recognizes MAQ Software as a Partner with Gold competencies in Data Analytics, Data Platform, and Application Development which certifies our expertise in delivering solutions based on the latest Microsoft products.

Amazon Consulting Partner

MAQ Software is a Consulting Partner for the Amazon Web Services based on our technical expertise and verified client references.

Microsoft Preferred Supplier

MAQ Software is a preferred supplier for Microsoft since 2000. Our supplier status confirms the confidence Microsoft Corporation has in our quality and delivery model.

Puget Sound Business Journal's 100 Fastest-Growing Private Companies in Washington State

MAQ Software is recognized as one of the fastest growing private companies in Washington State for five consecutive years.

MAQ Software is also featured in the Hall of Fame (companies that have made to the list four or more years, the Business Journal acknowledges by adding them to the Hall of Fame).
